Who We Are

A Family Business Built on Doing the Job Right

Elite Roofing was founded in 2004 by brothers Marcus and Daniel Hart, who believed homeowners deserved a roofer they could actually trust — one who shows up, does the work properly, and stands behind it.

Two decades later, that same ethos drives every project we take on. We've grown into one of the region's most respected roofing contractors, with a team of over 40 skilled professionals and a track record of more than 3,400 completed roofs.

We don't subcontract. Every crew member who walks onto your property is a trained, vetted Elite Roofing employee — and every project is supervised by a senior foreman with at least 10 years of experience.
